Our Company

Who we are!

DFS Virtual Call Center is a subsiduary of Digital Footprint Solutions, Inc. DFS was incorporated back in June 2017, in Fort Lauderdale Florida. Originally, DFS operated as a marketing company providing services such as Facebook Paid Marketing, Google Ads, SEO, and Website Development.

In 2017 we saw the need to expand our reach to a totally different demographic, and after much research, we decided to add a Call Center to the mix. Ofcourse, not just a brick and mortar call center, but one that is 100% remote and would be able to provide anyone who wishes to work from home, the opportunity to do so and still earn as much as a conventional 9 - 5 job.

This is where we partnered with Arise Virtual Solutions, Inc. As a B2B (Business to Business) model, this was perfect, and fit the goals of DFS. As you can see from the image provided, The Arise Platform has been and still is a leader in its field.

We began recruiting agents and within a month, we grew to 7 agents. Toay, we have over 75+ active working agents providing exceptional services to our Fortune 500 clients.
